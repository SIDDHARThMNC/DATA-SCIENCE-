# Project Scenarios & Use Cases

## Day 5 - Advanced ML Projects

### 1. Movie Streaming Clustering
**Scenario:** Streaming platform wants to segment 1000 users for personalized recommendations
**Features:** Watch time, devices, subscription pauses, genre preferences
**Goal:** Identify binge-watchers, casual viewers, at-risk cancelers
**Method:** K-Means with Elbow Method and Silhouette Score

### 2. Bank Customer Hierarchical Clustering  
**Scenario:** Retail bank segments customers for targeted financial products
**Features:** Age, Annual Income
**Goal:** Create customer segments (Young Starters, High-Income, etc.)
**Method:** Hierarchical clustering with dendrogram analysis

### 3. Diabetes Risk Prediction
**Scenario:** Healthcare provider predicts diabetes risk from BMI data
**Features:** BMI (Body Mass Index)
**Goal:** Early intervention for at-risk patients
**Method:** Logistic Regression classification

### 4. Email Spam Detection
**Scenario:** Email service filters spam messages
**Features:** Email text content
**Goal:** Classify emails as spam or legitimate
**Method:** Logistic Regression with text features

### 5. Insurance Claim Prediction
**Scenario:** Insurance company predicts claim likelihood
**Features:** Customer demographics, policy details
**Goal:** Risk assessment and fraud detection
**Method:** Logistic Regression

### 6. Employee Segmentation
**Scenario:** HR department groups employees for targeted programs
**Features:** Performance, experience, department
**Goal:** Identify high performers, training needs
**Method:** Hierarchical clustering

### 7. Customer Segmentation
**Scenario:** Retail business segments customers for marketing
**Features:** Purchase behavior, demographics
**Goal:** Targeted marketing campaigns
**Method:** K-Means clustering

### 8. Hospital Patient Segmentation
**Scenario:** Hospital groups patients for resource allocation
**Features:** Age, condition severity, treatment type
**Goal:** Optimize care delivery
**Method:** K-Means clustering

### 9. Telecom Clustering
**Scenario:** Telecom company segments customers to reduce churn
**Features:** Usage patterns, billing, complaints
**Goal:** Identify at-risk customers
**Method:** K-Means clustering

### 10. Loan Approval Decision Tree
**Scenario:** Bank automates loan approval decisions
**Features:** Income, credit score, employment
**Goal:** Fair and consistent loan decisions
**Method:** Decision Tree classification

### 11. University Admission
**Scenario:** University predicts admission likelihood
**Features:** GPA, test scores, extracurriculars
**Goal:** Streamline admission process
**Method:** Decision Tree
